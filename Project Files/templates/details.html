About Rice GrainPalette

Advanced AI technology for rice grain classification using deep learning

Deep Learning Technology

Our system uses a sophisticated Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on thousands of rice grain images. The model can identify subtle differences in grain shape, size, and texture that distinguish different rice varieties.

Technical Specifications

  • Model Architecture: Custom CNN with multiple convolutional layers
  • Input Resolution: 224x224 pixels
  • Training Data: Thousands of labeled rice grain images
  • Accuracy: Over 85% classification accuracy
  • Processing Time: Real-time inference (under 2 seconds)

Rice Varieties

Our system can accurately classify five major rice varieties, each with distinct characteristics:

Arborio Rice

Short-grain rice from Italy, characterized by its high starch content and ability to absorb liquids while maintaining a creamy texture. Ideal for risotto preparation.

Basmati Rice

Long-grain aromatic rice from the Indian subcontinent. Known for its distinctive fragrance, fluffy texture when cooked, and elongated grains that don't stick together.

Ipsala Rice

Turkish rice variety with medium-sized grains. Popular in Turkish cuisine for its good cooking qualities and ability to absorb flavors well in pilaf dishes.

Jasmine Rice

Fragrant long-grain rice from Thailand. Features a subtle floral aroma and slightly sticky texture when cooked, making it perfect for Asian cuisine.

Karacadag Rice

Premium Turkish rice variety grown in the Karacadag region. Known for its exceptional quality, distinctive taste, and cooking characteristics.

Image Guidelines

For best classification results, please follow these guidelines:

Use clear, well-lit images
Ensure rice grains are clearly visible
Avoid blurry or low-resolution images
Include multiple grains in the image
Use neutral background when possible
